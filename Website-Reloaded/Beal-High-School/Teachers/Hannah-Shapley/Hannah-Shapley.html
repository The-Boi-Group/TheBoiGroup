Hannah Shapley

Hannah Shapley is a contemporary artist based in East London. Primarily working with acrylic, her style draws influence from both urban and natural landscapes. Hannah Shapley (b. 1989 Cornwall) graduated with a BA from Plymouth University and is currently working as a Fine Art teacher in East London.
